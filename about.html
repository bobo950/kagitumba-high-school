About Kagitumba High School

Building Excellence Since 1996

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To continue framing and solve the greatest challenges of the future based on sciences and technology.

Vision

We develop well-rounded and thoughtful students prepared to core with changing post-modern, technological and globalized world.

Core Values

  • ✓ Responsibility - Accountability for our actions and choices
  • ✓ Harmony - Working together in unity and cooperation
  • ✓ Integrity - Honesty and ethical conduct in all interactions
  • ✓ Resilience - Perseverance and adaptability in facing challenges
  • ✓ Respect - Valuing diversity and inclusion in our community

Our Journey

1

1996 - Foundation

Kagitumba High School is established as a beacon of educational excellence in the region.

2

2010 - Expansion

New facilities added including science labs, modern library, and sports complex.

3

2015 - Innovation

Introduction of computer labs and digital learning resources to enhance education.

4

2026 - Excellence

Continuing commitment to academic excellence with state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated staff.
